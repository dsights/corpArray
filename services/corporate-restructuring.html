Mergers, Acquisitions, and Restructuring Compliance

In a dynamic and competitive business environment, corporate restructuring is often essential for survival and growth. Whether it's a merger to achieve synergies, an acquisition to expand market share, or a demerger to unlock value, these transactions are complex and require careful planning and execution. At CorpArray, we provide expert advisory services to help you navigate the intricacies of corporate restructuring.

A Strategic Partner in Your Business Transformation

Our team of legal, financial, and tax experts provides end-to-end support for all types of corporate restructuring transactions. We work closely with our clients to understand their strategic objectives and develop a customized solution that meets their specific needs. Our goal is to ensure that the transaction is structured in a tax-efficient and compliant manner, and that it creates long-term value for all stakeholders.

Our Corporate Restructuring Services

1. Mergers and Amalgamations

We provide comprehensive support for mergers and amalgamations, from the initial due diligence to the final approval of the scheme by the NCLT. Our services include valuation of shares, drafting of the scheme of amalgamation, and obtaining all necessary regulatory approvals.

2. Acquisitions and Takeovers

We advise both acquirers and target companies on all aspects of acquisitions and takeovers. Our services include due diligence, structuring of the transaction, negotiation of the share purchase agreement, and compliance with all applicable laws and regulations, including the Takeover Code.

3. Demergers and Hive-offs

We assist companies in the process of demerging or hiving off non-core assets or businesses. Our services include the valuation of the business to be demerged, drafting of the scheme of demerger, and obtaining all necessary approvals from the NCLT and other regulatory authorities.

4. Financial Restructuring

We provide expert advice on financial restructuring, including debt restructuring, refinancing, and raising of fresh capital. We work closely with banks, financial institutions, and investors to develop a sustainable financial plan that meets the needs of the business.
