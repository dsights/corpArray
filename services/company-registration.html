Our Registration Services

1. Choosing the Right Business Structure

The first and most important step in starting a business is choosing the right legal structure. We provide expert advice on the various types of business structures available in India, including private limited companies, public limited companies, LLPs, and partnership firms. We help you choose the structure that is best suited to your business needs and long-term goals.

2. Name Approval and Reservation

We assist in the process of obtaining approval for your proposed company or LLP name from the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA). We conduct a thorough search to ensure that the proposed name is unique and does not infringe on any existing trademarks.

3. Document Preparation and Filing

We prepare and file all the necessary documents for the incorporation of your company or LLP, including the Memorandum of Association (MOA), Articles of Association (AOA), and various other forms and declarations. We ensure that all documents are prepared accurately and filed in a timely manner.

4. Post-Incorporation Compliance

Once your company or LLP is incorporated, there are a number of post-incorporation compliances that need to be completed. We provide comprehensive support to ensure that you meet all these requirements, including the appointment of an auditor, opening of a bank account, and issuance of share certificates.
