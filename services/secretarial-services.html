Secretarial Services

Ensuring seamless corporate compliance.

Advisory and Compliance for Global Clients

In today's complex regulatory environment, the role of a company secretary has evolved from a mere record-keeper to a strategic advisor on corporate governance. At CorpArray, we offer a comprehensive suite of secretarial services designed to ensure that your company adheres to all statutory and regulatory requirements, allowing you to focus on your core business activities.

A Partner in Your Corporate Journey

Our team of qualified company secretaries and legal professionals provides end-to-end support, from the incorporation of a company to its ongoing management and compliance. We act as a trusted partner to our clients, providing timely and accurate advice on all matters related to corporate law and governance.

Key Areas of Our Secretarial Services

1. Company Incorporation and Setup

We provide fast and efficient services for the incorporation of all types of companies, including private limited, public limited, and limited liability partnerships (LLPs). Our services include obtaining all necessary approvals, drafting the memorandum and articles of association, and filing all required documents with the Registrar of Companies (ROC).

2. Board and Shareholder Meeting Management

We assist in the convening and conduct of board and shareholder meetings, ensuring compliance with all legal and procedural requirements. Our services include the preparation of agendas, notices, and minutes, as well as providing advice on all matters to be discussed at the meetings.

3. Statutory Filings and Record Maintenance

We ensure that your company complies with all its statutory filing obligations, including the filing of annual returns, financial statements, and other documents with the ROC. We also assist in the maintenance of all statutory registers and records, ensuring that they are always up-to-date and available for inspection.

4. Corporate Governance Advisory

We provide expert advice on all aspects of corporate governance, helping you establish a framework that promotes transparency, accountability, and ethical conduct. Our services include the formulation of corporate governance policies, advice on the composition and functioning of the board, and assistance in the implementation of best practices.
