Our Corporate Litigation Services

1. Representation before the National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT)

The NCLT is a specialized quasi-judicial body that adjudicates on all matters related to company law. We represent clients in a wide range of matters before the NCLT, including insolvency and bankruptcy proceedings, oppression and mismanagement petitions, and mergers and amalgamations.

2. Appearances before the Registrar of Companies (ROC) and Regional Director (RD)

We represent clients in all their dealings with the ROC and RD, from routine filings and applications to responding to show-cause notices and defending against penal action. We ensure that our clients are always in compliance with the directives and orders of these regulatory authorities.

3. Shareholder Disputes

Disputes between shareholders can be disruptive and damaging to a business. We provide expert advice and representation in shareholder disputes, helping to resolve conflicts in a timely and cost-effective manner. We have extensive experience in handling cases of oppression and mismanagement, as well as disputes related to the transfer and valuation of shares.

4. Contractual and Commercial Disputes

We represent clients in a wide range of contractual and commercial disputes, including breach of contract, disputes over intellectual property, and recovery of debts. We provide strategic advice and effective representation to protect our clients' interests and achieve a favorable resolution.
