Stay Compliant with ASIC, Effortlessly

Every Australian company has annual obligations with ASIC. Missing deadlines can result in costly late fees and even company deregistration. CorpArray acts as your registered agent, ensuring your company remains compliant year-round.

What We Handle For You:

  • Annual Review Statement: We receive, review, and check your ASIC Annual Statement for accuracy.
  • Solvency Resolution: We prepare the required minute/resolution for Directors to sign declaring the company is solvent.
  • Fee Management: We remind you of the payment deadline for the annual review fee to avoid late penalties.
  • Change of Details: We lodge Form 484s for changes to addresses, officeholders, or shareholdings (within statutory timeframes).
  • Register Maintenance: We maintain your corporate register in accordance with the Corporations Act.

Why is this important?

ASIC late fees can range from $96 to $401 depending on how late the lodgement is. Furthermore, failure to pay annual review fees can lead to ASIC striking off (deregistering) your company, which freezes your bank accounts and assets.
