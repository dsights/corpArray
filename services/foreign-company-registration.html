Register Your Foreign Company in Australia

Expanding your business to Australia offers tremendous opportunities, but navigating the local regulatory landscape can be complex. CorpArray simplifies the process of registering a foreign company with the Australian Securities and Investments Commission (ASIC), ensuring full compliance so you can focus on growth.

Our Comprehensive Service Includes:

  • ASIC Registration: We handle all paperwork to register your foreign company (Form 402) and obtain your ARBN (Australian Registered Body Number).
  • Local Agent Services: Every foreign company must have a local agent. We act as your authorized representative to accept service of documents and ensure compliance.
  • Registered Office Address: Use our professional address as your registered office in Australia.
  • Tax Registration: Assistance with TFN (Tax File Number), ABN (Australian Business Number), and GST registration.

Why Choose CorpArray?

We specialize in helping international businesses establish a foothold in Australia. Our team understands the nuances of cross-border compliance and provides ongoing support to keep your entity in good standing.
