Corporate Audit

Ensuring compliance and financial integrity.

Secretarial, FEMA, and Stock Broking Audits

Corporate audits are a vital tool for ensuring compliance, identifying risks, and promoting good corporate governance. At CorpArray, we offer a range of specialized audit and assurance services designed to provide stakeholders with the confidence that the company is operating in a compliant and ethical manner.

A Comprehensive and Risk-Based Approach

Our audit methodology is based on a deep understanding of the business and the regulatory environment in which it operates. We adopt a risk-based approach, focusing on the areas that are most critical to the company's success and compliance. Our goal is not just to identify problems but to provide practical and actionable recommendations for improvement.

Key Areas of Our Corporate Audit Services

1. Secretarial Audit

A secretarial audit is a comprehensive review of a company's compliance with all applicable corporate and other laws. It is a mandatory requirement for all listed companies and certain other public companies. Our team of qualified company secretaries conducts a thorough and independent audit, providing the board with the assurance that the company is in full compliance with its legal and regulatory obligations.

2. FEMA Audit

A FEMA audit is a specialized review of a company's compliance with foreign exchange laws. It is a critical tool for identifying and mitigating the risks associated with cross-border transactions. Our team of FEMA experts conducts a detailed audit of all foreign exchange transactions, providing a comprehensive report on the company's compliance with FEMA and its various rules and regulations.

3. Stock Broking Audit

Stockbrokers are subject to a stringent regulatory framework designed to protect the interests of investors. We conduct a comprehensive audit of the operations and compliance of stockbrokers, ensuring that they are in full compliance with all SEBI and stock exchange regulations. Our audit covers all aspects of the stockbroking business, from client registration to trade execution and settlement.
