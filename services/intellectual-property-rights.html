Our IPR Services

1. Trademark Registration and Protection

A trademark is a unique identity that distinguishes your products or services from those of your competitors. We provide end-to-end services for trademark registration, from conducting a comprehensive search to filing the application and handling all prosecution matters. We also provide services for the enforcement of your trademark rights, including sending cease and desist notices and initiating legal action against infringers.

2. Copyright Registration and Enforcement

Copyright protects original works of authorship, such as literary, dramatic, musical, and artistic works. We provide services for the registration of copyrights, as well as for the enforcement of your rights against infringement. We also advise on all aspects of copyright law, including licensing and assignment of rights.

3. Patent Filing and Prosecution

A patent protects new and useful inventions. We provide comprehensive services for the filing and prosecution of patent applications in India and other countries. Our team of patent attorneys has expertise in a wide range of technical fields, enabling us to provide high-quality services to clients in various industries. We also provide services for the enforcement of patent rights, including litigation against infringers.
