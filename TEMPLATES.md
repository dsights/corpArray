## üìÑ Proposal Template Structure

1.  **Cover Page:** Logo, Client Name, Date, "Prepared by: [Your Name]".
2.  **Executive Summary:** 1-paragraph overview of their problem and your solution.
3.  **Scope of Work:** Detailed bullet points of exactly what is included.
    *   *e.g., For Company Reg: Name reservation, ASIC Form 201, Constitution, ABN Application.*
4.  **Timeline:** When work starts and estimated completion.
5.  **Investment:** Clear pricing table. Total Cost + GST.
6.  **Terms:** Payment terms (e.g., 50% upfront, 50% on completion).
7.  **Next Steps:** "To proceed, please sign below and return..."
